# Privacy Policy for Camera Reactions

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Overview

Camera Reactions is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our application handles your data.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Camera Reactions processes all video and gesture recognition locally on your device. No video, images, or usage data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

## How the App Works

1. **Video Processing**: Your webcam video is processed entirely on your local device
2. **Gesture Recognition**: Hand gesture detection happens locally using MediaPipe
3. **Virtual Camera**: Video output is sent to your local OBS Virtual Camera
4. **No Cloud Services**: No data is transmitted to any cloud service or remote server

## Data Storage

- No user data is stored by the application
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms are used
- No analytics or telemetry data is collected

## Permissions

Camera Reactions requires the following permissions:

- **Webcam Access**: To capture video for gesture recognition
- **Microphone Access**: For pass-through audio to the virtual camera (optional)
- **Internet Client**: For checking for updates (no user data transmitted)

## Third-Party Components

Camera Reactions uses the following open-source libraries:

- MediaPipe (Google): For hand gesture recognition (runs locally)
- OpenCV: For video processing (runs locally)
- PyQt5: For user interface
- OBS Virtual Camera: For virtual camera functionality

These components process data locally and do not transmit information to external servers.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document.

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All processing happens locally on your device and under your control.
